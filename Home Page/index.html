ABOUT INSTITUTION

Bharath Institute of Science and Technology (BIST)

Bharath Institute of Science and Technology (BIST) commenced its educational journey as the pioneering self-financing Engineering College in Tamil Nadu in 1984, under the visionary leadership of Dr. S. Jagathrakshakan and the auspices of Sri Lakshmi Ammal Educational Trust. The institution's commitment to academic excellence led to the establishment of Sree Balaji Dental College and Hospital in 1989. Recognizing the significant contributions, Sree Balaji Dental College and Hospital attained the status of a Deemed to be University by the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) in July 2002, operating under Section 3 of the UGC Act 1956. This marked the inception of the Bharath Institute of Higher Education and Research (BIHER), a new trust overseeing the university.

Bharath Institute of Science and Technology (BIST) and several other institutions were subsequently brought under the aegis of Bharath Institute of Higher Education and Research (BIHER). The institution has evolved into a global center for education, catering to the diverse needs of nearly 10,000 students from across the globe. Situated on a vast expanse of 603 acres, with 1.3 crore sq. ft of modern infrastructure spread across 6 campuses in Tamil Nadu and Puducherry, BIHER is committed to providing multi-stream, multi-cultural, and high-quality education.

The institution boasts state-of-the-art facilities, creating an environment conducive to learning and research. A qualified and dedicated teaching faculty is in place to guide students pursuing various undergraduate and postgraduate programs. BIHER stands as a beacon of education, fostering a dynamic and enriching academic experience for students worldwide.


ABOUT DEPARTMENT

School of Computing

The School of Computing, Bharath Institute of Higher Education and Research, encompasses the Department of Computer Science and Engineering and the Department of Information Technology. The Department of Computer Science and Engineering offers B.Tech. Computer Science and Engineering with NBA accreditation, B.Tech. Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence), B.Tech. Computer Science and Engineering (Cyber Security), M.Tech. Computer Science and Engineering. The Department of Information Technology provides B.Tech Information Technology and M.Tech Information Technology.

The school also offers Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering and Ph.D. in Information Technology. It has established collaborations with top-notch industries like IBM, Oracle, VMWare, RedHat, and Cisco to share knowledge and experience with students. The school houses a Centre of Excellence in digital forensics and Artificial Intelligence to bridge the gap between industry and academia.

Placements have been consistently above 90% for the past three years, with students securing positions with the highest salary of 24 lakhs per annum and an average salary of 3.5 lakhs per annum. The school provides guidance for placements starting from the first year. Notably, the students and faculty of the School of Computing have accumulated a total of 50 patents. Students are encouraged to be part of various Student Development Clubs to hone skills aligned with their passions.



ABOUT CONFERENCE

International Conference on Intelligent Computing and communication (ICICC 24)

The International Conference on Intelligent Computing and Communication (ICICC 24) will be held through physical mode. This conference marks the inaugural edition of the ICICC 24 series, commencing in the year 2024. The vision behind organizing this conference is to provide an excellent forum for researchers, scientists, and industrialists from interdisciplinary areas to showcase their current contributions in the recent area of Computer Science.

The objectives of ICICC 24 are to abreast the young minds from Institutes, Universities, Colleges, and Industries across the country with the latest advancements in the field of this area and to provide them with an opportunity to share their recent research and views in various fields of Computer Science and their applications for the ultimate benefit of the Society and Industry. The broad aim of the conference is to groom young engineers, researchers, and faculty members' minds to face the challenges of the future for effective and efficient professional responsibilities. The conference will cover a wide range of topics in the form of plenary speakers, keynote speakers, invited talks & contributory papers/poster presentations.

Scope of the Conference

The Scope of this International Conference on Intelligent Computing and Communication (ICICC 24) is to provide a forum to participants, an opportunity to discuss and exchange the recent developments in the field of Computer Science and Engineering and also review the challenges faced by the industry. The major objectives of the conference are:

  1. Instigate a research platform to bring academic researchers, scientists, and research scholars together to discuss and share their findings, innovations, and concerns in the fields of artificial intelligence, Computing, Machine learning, Internet of Things, BlockChain Technology, and Communication Network.
  2. Build collaborative links between academic researchers, scientists, and research scholars with national and international institutes.
  3. Support researchers to present and publish their innovative ideas and research output in reputed journals.
  4. Present a communication medium between academia and the industry sectors to discuss emerging trends, technologies, and challenges in the relevant research fields.
